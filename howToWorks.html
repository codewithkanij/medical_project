HOW IT WORKS

MEMBERSHIP FEES

Carolina, I finally found an opportunity to pursue this lifelong dream. Eventually, I attended Lake Erie College of Osteopathic Medicine (LECOM) in Bradenton, FL and graduated in 2012.

I completed my Internal Medicine residency at the beautiful East Tennessee State University in June 2015 and subsequently joined Halifax Health Medical Center as one of the hospitalists. I also teach FSU medical students.

I became particularly interested to start a concierge primary care so that I am able to focus on prevention at various levels instead of only providing acute care in the hospital.

In this concierge practice model, we will do our best to help with all primary care needs. I am interested in prevention based on current evidence as well as our shared goals. As we build our practice, we will systematically incorporate evidence-based practices to achieve these goals.

INSURANCE MATTERS

Our current plan is to operate on yearly membership fees. Membership fees for concierge practice are not covered by insurance companies or medicaid/medicare.

More importantly, it is vital to keep health insurance as it may be necessary for any hospital admission, ER visits, and to see other healthcare providers.

HOSPITAL COVERAGE

I have access to see patients at Halifax Daytona Beach, Halifax Port Orange, and UF Health/Halifax Deltona.

If you are admitted to any of these three facilities, I will either be involved directly in your care or will coordinate care with our hospital providers.
